Top 5 Tourist Destinations in Kerala

Munnar-The Kashmir of South India

Sprawling tea plantations surround the serene hills of Munnar, which attract adventure travellers hungry for paragliding, treks to Anaimudi (South India's highest peak) and hikes originating at the confluence of three mountain streams.

Alappuzha-The Venice of East

Often compared to Venice due to its abundance of canals, Alappuzha is the gateway to the backwaters of Kerala. Alappuzha, famous for its boat races, beaches, marine products and coir industry, is a world renowned backwater tourist destination of India.


Learn More

Wayanad-The Ooty of Kerala

Wayanad, the green paradise is nestled among the mountains of the Western Ghats, forming the border world of the greener part of Kerala. Clean and pristine, enchanting and hypnotizing, this land is filled with history and culture. This verdant hill station is full of plantations, forests and wildlife.


Learn More

Kovalam-Paradise of the South

Kovalam is the most famous beach spot of Kerala. Tourists from all over the world approach these beaches for sun-bathing, swimming in shallow waters and catamaran cruising.


Learn More

Thekkady

Nestled amidst the rolling hills and thick forests of Kerala, Thekkady is a place blessed with the bounties of nature. It is perfect for those looking to escape the monotony of city life!
